Frequently Asked Questions FAQ

Where should I go on my first day?

Arrive at the studio at 9:00am. You can park in the adjacent parking lot in front of the building

What do I need to bring on my first day?

Make sure to bring all of your paperwork, a copy of your driver's license, and a smile!

What should I wear?

Anything you feel comfortable in! We want this to be your second home and we are open to all styles and comfort levels. We just ask that you shower.

What is New Hire Orientatin and when is it?

New Hire Orientation is a two day event where you will meet other new hires, get your computer, meet your team, and learn the layout of the building. There will also be speakers who can shed some light on how this gem of a company came into existance.

New Hire Orientation will take place on your first and second day from 9:00am - 4:00pm in the Annex building.
